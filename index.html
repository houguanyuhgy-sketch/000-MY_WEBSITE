Artist Statement
"My artistic practice is rooted in a deep reflection on the digital age. I believe generative art has evolved beyond mere technical exploration to become a core mode of expression for our time. Code, as the foundational building material of the 21st century, not only reshapes human interaction, creation, and cognition but also opens up new frontiers for art. My work is a response to this profound societal transformation, aiming to explore the unique translation of traditional artistic vocabularies into a modern digital context."
我的艺术实践根植于对数字时代的深刻反思。生成艺术已不再是单纯的技术探索，而是我们这个时代的核心表达方式。代码，作为21世纪的基础建材，不仅重构了人类的社交、创造与认知，也为艺术开辟了全新的疆域。我的创作，便是对这场深刻社会变革的回应，旨在探索传统艺术语汇在现代数字语境下的独特转译。
"My creations are born from a deep-seated medium-awareness, fusing the color theory of Neo-Impressionism with the spirit of Mec-art. Using p5.js code, I deconstruct images into their most fundamental visual unit—the dot—and reassemble them through algorithmic logic. This approach is both a tribute to historical art techniques and a practice built upon a profound understanding of the digital medium's nature."
作品是对创作媒介本身的一次深刻自觉，融合了新印象派的色彩理论与机械艺术（Mec-art）的精神。我通过p5.js代码，将图像解构成最基础的视觉单元——"点"，并以算法的逻辑重组它们。这既是向历史艺术技法的致敬，也是基于对数字媒介本质的深刻理解。
"I hope my art acts as a bridge connecting nature and algorithm, tradition and the future. I invite the audience to witness, within these worlds made of dots, how the simplest elements can build a universe brimming with emotion and dynamism, and to find their own moments of contemplation and connection within them."
我希望我的作品能成为一个桥梁，连接着自然与算法、传统与未来。我邀请观众在这些由"点"构成的世界里，见证最简单的元素如何构建出充满情感与动态的宇宙，并从中找到属于自己的那份沉思与感动。
"All digital artworks are best presented as museum-quality Giclée prints on heavyweight, archival paper, which would give their intricate digital textures a tangible quality and depth."
所有数字作品都适合以博物馆典藏级的艺术微喷（Giclée Print）形式，呈现在重磅无酸的档案纸上，使其细腻的数字纹理具有可触摸的质感与深度。
